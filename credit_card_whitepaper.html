Credit Card Processing Options for a Small Webstore

PCI Compliance

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is a security policy that any company storing cardholder data must implement. It is very restrictive and too expensive for a small company to implement. Entire document: https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/document_library?category=pcidss&document=pci_dss

Summary
  • Build and Maintain a Secure Network and Systems
    1. Install and maintain a firewall

      The company must secure every connection to the internet with a firewall, including firewalls on every device used by employees.

    2. Do not use vendor-supplied defaults

      This means setting up and maintaining secure passwords, and keeping security configurations relevant for the companies system.
