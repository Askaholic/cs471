Credit Card Processing Options for a Small Webstore

PCI Compliance

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is a security policy that any company storing cardholder data must adhere to. It is very restrictive and not affordable for a small company to implement. View entire document: https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/document_library?category=pcidss&document=pci_dss

Summary

  • Build and Maintain a Secure Network and Systems
    1. Install and maintain a firewall
    2. Do not use vendor-supplied defaults
